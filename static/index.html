This map tracks bicycle and pedestrian collisions in El Cerrito, Richmond, Albany, and Berkeley — comparing official SWITRS records against EMS dispatch radio transcripts to measure how many crashes go unreported.

Data sources

SWITRS (currently shown) — California's official Statewide Integrated Traffic Records System, exported from tims.berkeley.edu. Covers only crashes that were reported to law enforcement.

EMS Dispatch (limited data — still processing) — extracted from Broadcastify scanner archive recordings using Whisper transcription and Claude, plus news reports. Reveals crashes that were responded to by EMS but never filed as police reports.

Methodology

About 40% of SWITRS records lack GPS coordinates and are geocoded using Claude (address normalization) and the Google Maps API. Geocoded incidents are validated against a West Contra Costa bounding box.
